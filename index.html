About

I’m a Computer Science student with a minor in Graphic Design, focused on building software that is both technically strong and user-centered. I’ve worked on real-time systems at NIWC Pacific, developing GUI tools and computer vision features using Python and OpenCV, and have also contributed to web and design-focused projects through Omens.agency. Currently, I’m gaining experience in a professional engineering environment at QuidelOrtho, continuing to refine my skills in development, collaboration, and production-level systems. I enjoy creating applications that balance performance, usability, and clean design.

When I'm not coding or designing, I'm usually playing video-games or hanging out with friends.
